Definitions
Client Every person, adults and children, that are served by an HMIS participating organization.
Continuum of Care The local, HUD defined, community bound together for federal grant data collection and reporting.
Head of Household/Primary Client The primary person responsible or involved in obtaining services from an organization. Not related to any other federal definition such as IRS.
Household One or more persons obtaining services from an organization. A single individual is considered a household of 1. A person can belong to more than pne household.
Organization An agency, church, or other entity that provides services to the community.
Program A government defined grant partner.
Project A single or related group of services offerred by an organization. For example, a church may offer counseling services and monetary help with household bills. Each of these would be considered and tracked as a project.
Project Entry This tracks when a client starts receiving services from a particular project.
Project Exit his tracks when a client "exits" or no longer requires a given project's services.
Universal Data Element (UDE) These are data elements that are required of every client entered into HMIS - regardless of the program, project, or organization.
Project Type A general description of a project. The valid types are:
emergency shelter
transitional houing
permanent housing
permanent supportive housing
street outreach
services only
other
safe haven
day shelter
homeless prevention
rapid rehousing
coordinated assessment
Service Provider An organization, agency, church, local government branch, etc. that offers services to the community.
